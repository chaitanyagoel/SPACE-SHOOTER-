  • 'SPACE' bar! (Shoot the plane by bullet)
  • 'B' key (Shoot the plane by bomb 20 available)

  • 'I' (increase y velocity)
  • 'J' (decrease x velocity)

  • 'L' (increase x velocity)

  • By 'P' key or by button given on the screen , You can pause the game

  • After certain kills , new spaceships will be added .
  • Different spaceship , decrease your health score differently .
